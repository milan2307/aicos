# The Offer: AI Chief of Staff for Builders

## The Problem

Ambitious solo builders and entrepreneurs have too much information and not enough clarity.

They read newsletters, follow AI Twitter, have five ideas running in parallel, and still end their day unsure whether they worked on the right thing. They know AI can help. They do not know how to build a system around it.

Milan built that system for himself over six weeks. It tells him exactly what to work on each morning, scores opportunities before he commits to them, and synthesises insights from multiple agents into one daily recommendation. It has caught him working on the wrong thing (37/50 conviction score vs 46/50 for the right one).

Other builders want this. They cannot build it.

---

## The Service

**AICOS delivers a done-for-you AI Chief of Staff.**

Milan runs a set of AI agents customised to each client's goals and context. The agents generate a daily brief and weekly synthesis. Milan reviews the output, makes sure it is grounded in the client's real situation, and delivers it on a fixed schedule.

The client gets clarity. Milan handles the system.

---

## What Clients Actually Receive

### Daily Brief (every weekday, before 8am local time)

```
Good Morning, [Name] — Monday, June 23, 2026

Top 3 Priorities for Today
1. [Specific, context-aware action — not generic advice]
2. [Second priority]
3. [Third priority]

Strategic Note
[One paragraph on what matters most this week and why]

One Decision to Make Today
[A specific decision framed with two clear options]

Operating Reminder
[One sentence tied to their stated goals and style]
```

### Weekly Synthesis (every Friday, before 5pm local time)

- Progress review against 90-day goals
- What is working (with specific evidence from the week)
- What is stalling (honest, not motivational)
- Priorities for next week (ranked)
- One strategic recommendation
- One question to sit with over the weekend

### Monthly Review *(Professional and Executive only)*

- Strategic health check: are they moving toward the goal or drifting?
- Opportunity scoring: what should they be building in the next 90 days?
- One major recommendation with reasoning
- Updated 90-day goal if needed
