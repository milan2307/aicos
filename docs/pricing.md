# Pricing

## Tiers

### Starter — $299/month

For builders who want daily clarity and weekly direction.

- Daily AI Chief of Staff brief (weekdays)
- Weekly synthesis (every Friday)
- Onboarding call (30 minutes, one-time)
- Context update whenever their situation changes

**Best for:** Solo founders, indie hackers, freelancers with clear goals.

---

### Professional — $499/month

For builders who want deeper strategic support.

Everything in Starter, plus:
- Monthly strategic review
- One async question per week (Milan reviews with the agents, responds within 24h)
- Priority response time

**Best for:** Consultants, small agency owners, builders with multiple active projects.

---

### Executive — $799/month

For builders who want the full system.

Everything in Professional, plus:
- 30-minute monthly call with Milan
- Opportunity scoring on demand (submit an opportunity, agents score it within 48h)
- Custom agent configuration tuned to their specific workflow

**Best for:** Founders with real revenue who need an AI Chief of Staff running alongside them, not just delivering briefs.

---

## Notes

**No annual lock-in.** Month to month. Cancel any time.

**First month is discounted 50% for founding clients** (first 5 clients only). This is not a permanent discount — it is recognition that the first clients are helping shape the service. They pay $150/$250/$400 for month one, then full price from month two.

**Payment:** Invoice on the 1st of each month. Bank transfer or card.
